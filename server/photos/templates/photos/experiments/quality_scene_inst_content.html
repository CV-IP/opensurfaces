Frequently asked questions

If the picture is primarily of people, not the specific room, do we still click on it?

As long as the people are in the room, select the photo. If there are so many people that you cannot tell what room they are in, don't select the photo.

What it looks plausible, but I'm not certain?

Don't click the photo if you aren't certain.

Do we count industrial or restaurant kitchens?

Both types of kitchens count as "kitchen".

Does it count as a kitchen if there are pots and pans but not the actual room?

No. You should see some of the surrounding room that suggests that the pots are in a kitchen.


Why are we doing this?

We are trying to build a database of materials. This experiment is part of ongoing research at Cornell University. Thank you for participating!
